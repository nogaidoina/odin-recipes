Neapolitan Pizza Dough

Description:

Neapolitan pizza, also known as Naples-style pizza, is a style of pizza made with tomatoes and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Flour(00) 1 kg
  • Dry yeast 1 tsp
  • Water 600 ml
  • Salt 5 tsp
  • Olive Oil 2 tsp

Steps:

  1. In stand mixer put water dan 10% of flour mix 1 min.
  2. Add yeast mix 2 min to activate yeast.
  3. Add half of remaining flour, mix slow and increase speed, add rest of flour slowly. Add half of remaining flour, mix slow and increase speed add rest of flour slowly.
  4. Add salt and mix for another 5 min.
  5. Add extra virgin olive oil and mix until incorporate about 3 min.
  6. We make the dough into a ball, and put it in a large bowl, covering the bowl with plastic wrap to prevent air from entering. Rest the dough for 16-20 h in the fridge.
  7. Put the dough on the floured table, divide the dough into portions of 250 g each.
  8. Make pizza bowls and put them in the floured pizza tray. Live at he room temperature for 4 h.
  9. Can be used within a week kept in the fridge. Take out of the fridge for 4 h for use.
  10. Cook at 400 C for 90 s.
